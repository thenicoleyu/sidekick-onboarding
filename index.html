Stay safe with Sidekick

With one tap, Sidekick will call 911 and alert your emergency contacts.

Building a safer community with technology

Sidekick aims to keep women and children travelling alone at night safe. Sidekick keeps your emergency contacts updated of your status and, if you're ever in danger, immediately takes action.

Using Sidekick is Easy

Step 1.
Tap "Start Tracking"

Step 2.
Tap SOS if you're in danger

Once you trigger an SOS alert:

1. Sidekick calls 911

2. Texts your emergency contacts

3. Records live audio & video

Keep your child safe

Once your child starts a tracking session, you can use Sidekick to stay connected with your child in real time.
